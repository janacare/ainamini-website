Disease Management

  • Home Monitoring

    The smartphone connected Aina Device, enables your patients to seamlessly monitor blood glucose, receive timely support for hypoglycemia and also connect daily glucose variations with their diet and physical activity.

  • Behaviour Coaching

    The Habits app comes integrated with the Aina Device. It is a virtual diabetes coach that helps patients reach their diet and exercise goals through personalized daily checklists which includes tips, tasks, lessons and quizzes.

  • Hypoglycemia Support

    Timely support and suggestions to help patients deal with hypoglycemia episodes

  • Physician Dashboard

    Complete your diabetes practice with the Habits physician dashboard. See patient progress, lab data, medications, and clinical data all in one place.
