Our Solution

NT-proBNP assisted Congestive Heart Failure Management Program (Launching in South Asia in Fall 2017)

  • Habits Heart

    Patient receives personalized daily checklist with a set of tasks, lessons and tips.

  • Test NT-proBNP

    Checklist prompts the patient to check NT-proBNP using the Aina Device.

  • Symptom survey gets triggered

    Based on the NT-proBNP value, patient is prompted to take a symptom survey and contact his or her doctor immediately.

  • Patient Education

    Patient receives a 2-minute lesson on "identifying and managing symptoms" right after this episode to manage such situations in the future.
